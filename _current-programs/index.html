LU Programs Around the Country

LU programs are currently running or starting up at over 30 universities in over 12 states and the District of Columbia, as well as one international program in the UK. Click on a program near you for more information.

Current Programs

Rainstorm is our new online Splash to facilitate the same excitement our in-person programs do during the COVID-19 crisis. The program is run by undergraduate student leaders at some of the following Splashes, as well as by the volunteer staff of Learning Unlimited.
