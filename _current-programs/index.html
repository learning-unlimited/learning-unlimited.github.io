LU Programs Around the Country

Learning Unlimited programs have run at over 50 universities and schools across over a dozen states. Click on a program near you for more information!


Current Programs

On 4/9, Brandeis ESS welcomes local high-school and middle-school students to Brandeis University campus for their Splash program!

On 4/9, Princeton Splash holds their eponymous spring program!

On 4/10, Boston College Splash offers a wide variety of courses to middle- and high-school students!

On 4/16, Splash at Yale holds its flagship spring program for high-school and middl-school students grades 7-12!

On 4/16, UC Berkeley Splash hosts its 15th Splash program, where high-school students are invited to UC Berkeley’s campus!

On 4/16, Cornell Splash brings any student age 13 and above to Cornell’s virtual campus!

On 4/23, Hopkins Splash invites students in grades 9-12 to attend a virtual Splash event!

Also, check out Rainstorm, a free online Splash program offered twice a year by volunteers of Learning Unlimited!
