Impact

Our Results

Learning Unlimited's programs have reached well over 20,000 students, with an estimated program attendance of over 10,000 for the 2013–2014 academic year alone. We continue to shape the way thousands of students look at education.

20k+ Students reached total
10k+ Program attendance in 2013–14 alone
2,000 College students taught last year

In addition, approximately 2,000 undergraduate and graduate students taught for a Splash program in the past academic year, providing crucial experience communicating ideas and sharing their passions. After our programs, we conduct a survey of participants to measure their response to the program. The results are both informative and motivating.
